Te Whare Tapa Wha

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Light exercise (at least 30 minutes) 4 times per week

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Talk to parents at least once a week

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Pick up at least sport again by the end of the year

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Taking time to express gratitude for what I have each day

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Get outside for "fresh air" at least once a day
