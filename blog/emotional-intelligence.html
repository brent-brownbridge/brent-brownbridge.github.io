Emotional Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the knowledge of ones own and others feelings and emotions and using this knowledge critically to guide your own actions and thought process.

How is EQ different to IQ?.

Emotional intelligence is the use of feelings and emotions to guide your thinking and actions, whereas in intelligence quotient is the use of problem solving to represent ones reasoning abilities.

Why is EQ important?

Good EQ can lead to a better understanding of the workplace, study, and people around you. Having a better emotional understanding of what's going on around you can aid in self confidence, and happiness, which can increase drive and standards and overall increase ones performance at work or whilst studying.
